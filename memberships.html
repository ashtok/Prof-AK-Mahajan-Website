Memberships & Affiliations

Professor Ambrish Kumar Mahajan has been an active member of various prestigious professional societies, committees, and academic institutions throughout his distinguished career in geology. These affiliations reflect his commitment to advancing geological sciences and sharing expertise across various platforms.

Government & Academic Committees

National Disaster Management Authority

Expert Member, Seismic Vulnerability Assessment Committee

Led the development of comprehensive seismic microzonation methodologies for urban centers in seismically active regions. Contributed to national guidelines for earthquake risk assessment and mitigation strategies.

2012 - 2018
Key Contribution: Authored chapter on "Geological Inputs for Seismic Microzonation" in the national guidelines.

Bureau of Indian Standards

Technical Committee Member, Earthquake Engineering Sectional Committee

Participated in the revision of seismic codes and standards for building construction in India. Provided geological expertise for the assessment of site-specific seismic parameters and foundation conditions.

2010 - 2020
Key Contribution: Revision of IS 1893 (Criteria for Earthquake Resistant Design of Structures).

Ministry of Earth Sciences

Scientific Review Committee Member

Evaluated research proposals and projects related to geohazards, seismicity, and environmental geology. Provided recommendations for funding allocations and project implementation strategies.

2015 - Present
Key Contribution: Reviewed over 50 major research proposals in geological sciences.
